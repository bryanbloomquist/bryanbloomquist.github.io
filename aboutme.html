About Me

I joined the Army Reserves right out of high school so I could pay for my college education. My unit was activated during the Iraq War and I was deployed my senior year. After my deployment I finished my bachelors degree while working full time at the United States Postal Service. It was during those 14 years at the post office that I started my wonderful family and I had to make a choice: Stay with my union protected federal job and never see my kids, or, leave the post office when I'm almost 40 years old and learn a new career. Not that difficult of a decision, I became a full-time dad and a part-time student at the University of Minnesota Full-Stack Web Development Boot Camp. In boot camp, both for the Army and for Web Development, I learned that I love a challenge and I push myself to overcome all obstacles. I am a fast learner and enjoy working with others on tasks, and I really, really love coding. I am fastly developing skills in HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, and Bootstrap.
